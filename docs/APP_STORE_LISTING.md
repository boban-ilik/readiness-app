## Description *(4000 chars max)*

Know exactly how hard to push today — before you lace up.

Readiness connects to Apple Health and turns your health data into a daily readiness score from 0–100, a personalised AI briefing, and a 3-day training forecast. No extra hardware, no subscription device — works with the watch and gear you already own.

**WORKS WITH YOUR WHOLE HEALTH ECOSYSTEM**
Readiness pulls data from Apple Health, which means it works with virtually any device that syncs to it — Apple Watch, Garmin, Polar, Fitbit, Whoop, Samsung Galaxy Watch, Oura Ring, and more. If your device sends data to Apple Health, Readiness reads it. One coach for all your data, no matter what you wear.

**YOUR DAILY READINESS SCORE**
Every morning, Readiness analyses your overnight HRV, resting heart rate, sleep duration, sleep stages, stress index, and daytime activity to compute a score calibrated to your personal baselines — not a generic population average. 62 means something different for you than for someone else, and Readiness knows the difference.

**AN AI COACH THAT EXPLAINS EVERYTHING**
Your score is only as useful as what you do with it. That's why Readiness writes you a personalised daily briefing: what's happening in your body today, why your score is what it is, and exactly what to train — or not train — today.

"Your HRV has dropped 11ms over three consecutive days — a classic early-fatigue pattern. Skip the intervals today and you'll thank yourself by Thursday."

That's the kind of specific, data-grounded coaching that used to cost $30/month and a hardware subscription.

**3-DAY TRAINING FORECAST**
See your projected readiness for the next three days so you can rearrange your training schedule before fatigue hits — not after. Protect your key session. Nail your race-week taper. Stop guessing.

**PATTERN INTELLIGENCE**
The longer you use Readiness, the smarter it gets. After 30 days, it detects patterns unique to your physiology — HRV trends, sleep debt accumulation, recovery rebounds after hard weeks — and weaves them into your daily coaching. It learns your normal, not population averages.

**TAG YOUR DAYS**
Training doesn't happen in a vacuum. Tag days with life events — alcohol, illness, travel, high stress, poor sleep, intense workouts — and watch your coach connect the dots. "Your HRV dropped 14% the morning after you tagged a late night. This is a recurring pattern."

**CHAT WITH YOUR COACH**
Ask anything, anytime. "Should I do my long run tomorrow?" "Why is my HRV consistently low on Thursdays?" "What's my best intensity window this week?" Your AI coach has your full data history and answers in plain English — not charts.

**PRO FEATURES**
- Daily AI briefing written from your data every morning
- AI coach chat with full health context
- 3-day readiness forecast
- Pattern intelligence (30-day trend analysis)
- Full score breakdown: Recovery · Sleep · Stress
- Training load analysis from workouts
- Life event tagging to track what affects you

Free tier includes your daily readiness score and 7-day history — enough to see exactly what Readiness can do.
